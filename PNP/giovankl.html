Information

Dr. Giovanetti is part of the JMU PNP (Particle and Nuclear Physics) group. Dr K.Giovanetti works closely with Dr. I. Niculescu and Dr. Gabriel Niculescu supporting basic research that probes fundamental particle structure and interactions. The group maintains two well equipped N&P labs that provide support for ongoing projects at Jefferson Lab, Fermi Lab and PSI. These labs also serve as a training ground for undergraduates. The physics department at JMU has made undergraduate research a key component in the physics curriculum and the PNP group has a superb record at engaging students in exciting and educational experiences.
