JMU PNP Group

Our Research group is led by three full-time JMU staff. These professors, as well as leading and conducting research with the PNP group, also teach a number of classes every semester. They put much time and effort into making sure their students have an opportunity not only to learn the material they teach, but also to experience the science behind that material.

The PNP research group, as well as including three professors, also harbors the learning and research of a number of students. The 2010 summer includes six undergraduate and two graduate students in the research team. These students are given responsibility, with the supervision of the faculty professors, to complete many tasks related to the research projects, such as testing PMTs or building receptor modules.
